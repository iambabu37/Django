About Us

Antibacterial Compound Database for Bacteria (AnBac-DB) is a dedicated platform designed to compile, curate, and provide easy access to information on antibacterial compounds effective against various bacterial species. Our mission is to empower researchers, healthcare professionals, and students with a comprehensive, reliable, and user-friendly resource that accelerates the discovery and understanding of antibacterial agents.

  • Comprehensive Compound Catalog

    AnBac-DB features an extensive collection of natural and synthetic antibacterial compounds, including their chemical properties, sources, and documented bacterial targets.

  • Empowering Research & Innovation

    Our database is a powerful tool for researchers and drug developers, supporting the search for novel antibacterial agents and facilitating comparative studies across different bacterial pathogens.

  • Accessible & User-Friendly

    With intuitive search and filtering options, AnBac-DB makes it easy for users to find relevant compounds, explore their mechanisms of action, and stay updated with the latest advancements in antibacterial research.
